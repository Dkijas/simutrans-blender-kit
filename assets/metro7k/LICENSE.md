## A note on references

Unlike the serie 9000, this model was built **without a reference photograph in
the repository**: the livery layout was read off photographs seen elsewhere, and
the front colour and the pantograph position are assumed to match the 9000. See
`README.md` — they are not confirmed, and nothing here should be taken as a
measured record of the real train's appearance.
